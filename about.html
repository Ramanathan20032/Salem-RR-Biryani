We established in 1987

hard work, perseverance, and passion

Tamil Selvan [ Founder & Chairman ] , started selling homemade chicken biryani & chicken 65 from a small roadside stall in Broadway, Chennai. His dedication helped stabilize his business, increasing its volume steadily over the years.

The secret to his flavorful recipes? His mother's cherished recipe. This business later became Salem RR Biryani Pvt Ltd, a renowned brand among biryani lovers. The first eatery opened in 1998 in Salem with 8 seats. Its popularity led to a series of branches across the city.
