Sailing Towards The Best Biryani In Town

aromatic spices, authentic flavors

Salem RR Biryani is crafted with fragrant Basmati rice, marinated meat, and a blend of aromatic spices like cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon. Cooked in a traditional dum-style, each pot is sealed and slow-cooked for hours, allowing the flavors to deepen and the meat to turn tender and juicy.

Beyond biryani, we serve South Indian delights. From Chicken 65 to Chettinadu Pepper Chicken and Mutton Keema, our dishes deliver bold flavors. Enjoy a casual dining experience with spacious seating and warm hospitality.
